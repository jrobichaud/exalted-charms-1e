        p. 238). Activating a protocol is effectively activating a
        Combo, and every Exalt in the protocol must be eligible to
        do so, meaning one cannot have used another Charm or
        Combo this turn.<br>
        When one member of the protocol attempts to
        activate the protocol and the protocol is legal, every
        member of the protocol is instantly aware of the attempt

        access to all the Charms in the protocol.

        normally end that Charm. Characters cannot have more

        capability that permanently enhance the Exalt, such as
        Ox-Body Technique.<br>

        entation of the Terrestrial Exalted. Those Exalts who are

        develop these Combos. This Charm has no cost, save the
        costs of the protocols created with it, but instead, indicates

        The benefits lent by this Charm are bonus dice from
        a Martial Art and cannot exceed the character's Attribute
        + Ability on any one roll.
